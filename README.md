## 📌 What is claude-brief?

Claude-brief keeps your work organized while you use Claude Code. When you work on multiple tasks at the same time, it is easy to lose track of where you are. This tool creates a small, docked window. This window shows your current goal and key details. It stays visible as you work. You no longer need to switch tabs or dig through terminal history to remember your task. It acts as your external memory. It ensures you know your exact status across all your active work sessions.

## 🔧 Frequently Asked Questions

Click the standard close button at the top corner of the window. The application remains running in your system tray. You can reopen it by clicking the app icon in your taskbar.

### Does it require a network connection?
The app only needs a network connection during the download and installation process. You can use the software offline after it is installed on your computer.

### Will it interfere with my other apps?
The app is designed to be non-intrusive. It stays pinned to the edge of your screen. You can resize your other windows to sit next to it. It does not overlap with your workspace unless you drag it there.

### How do I uninstall the software?
